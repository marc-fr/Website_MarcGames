About the game

The player controls battleships, inspired from the World-War 2, in strategic battles.

Features

  • Play epic battles, with a challenging I.A
  • Fine control of every ships whenever you want
  • Dynamic weather conditions, that have an impact on the strategy

Screenshots

The game is currently a early prototype.
At the moment, I focus on the rendering part, with a full procedural atmospheric scattering and ocean simulation.

This game is inspired from a python game that I have made in the 2010's.
The game is actually almost unplayable, but I still kept the idea in mind.
