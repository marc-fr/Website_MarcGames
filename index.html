Moonlanding project

This is a skill game which I started making on my wonderful TI-82 calculator in 2005 during math lectures in high-school. In this game you pilot a spaceship and the goal is to land safely at the designated area with limited fuel... From my calculator to a proper PC, I extended the concept to a platform game, with a game-engine developed from scratch.

Tarot au bout

The French-Tarot is one of the ultimate card games with tactics and fears! This game is technically simple to use, it includes a challenging A.I., and there aren't any advert or network restriction. Simply have fun and practise to your heart's content...
