Moonlanding project

This is a skill game, at the beginning made on my wonderful TI-82 calculator in 2005, during high-school math lessons. You pilot a vessel, and the goal is land safely at the destination, but the fuel is limited... From my calculator to PCs, I extended the concept to a platform game, with a game-engine developed from scratch.
